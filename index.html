How to play BOMB.COM:

Welcome to your first day on the job as an FBI Agent!

Just as your luck would have it, your very first task is to defuse a bomb found near the White House. You have 2 minutes to defuse the bomb, or else... well... we'll leave that to your imagination. 🙃

⏳ To defuse the bomb: ⏳

  • Click on one of the four parts of the bomb to try and defuse that part.
  • Each part has a logic problem that will defuse that part of the bomb, once solved.
  • If you are having trouble solving a section of the bomb, tackle another part and come back later.
  • Once all four parts of the bomb are solved, the bomb will be defused.
